Cookie Notice

Last updated: April 2026

This page explains how EdgeHUD uses cookies on this website and what that means for your privacy.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work correctly and remember your preferences between visits.

What Cookies We Use

This website uses only one cookie: a functional cookie called cookieOk. It is set when you click "Got it" on the cookie notice banner. Its only purpose is to remember that you have dismissed the banner so it does not appear again on future visits.

This cookie does not track you, collect personal data, or share any information with third parties. It expires when you clear your browser storage.

What We Do Not Use

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts that collect data about your behaviour on this site. No data is sent to Google, Meta, or any other external service.

Your Rights Under GDPR and UK GDPR

Because we use only a strictly necessary functional cookie, we are not required to obtain your consent under the EU ePrivacy Directive or UK PECR. However, you remain in full control of your browser storage at all times.

Managing and Deleting Cookies

You can clear or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Removing the cookieOk cookie will cause the cookie banner to reappear on your next visit. It will not affect any other aspect of the site.

Instructions for common browsers:

Chrome — Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data
Firefox — Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data
Safari — Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
Edge — Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data

Changes to This Notice

If we change how we use cookies in the future, this page will be updated and the date above will reflect the most recent revision.
